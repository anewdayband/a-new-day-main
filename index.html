WE ARE A NEW DAY. 2025-2026

A New Day is a melodic metalcore band from South Texas formed by former members of the now-disbanded emo outfit Truant, drawing influence from the emotionally driven and powerful sounds of early 2000s bands like A Long Winter, Hopesfall, and Broken by Silence. Combining heaviness with melody and electronic textures.

Band Members:
Brenden Riker – Lead Vocalist
David Falcon – Guitarist
Elijah Vega – Drummer
Noah Benavidez – Guitarist
George Gonzales – Synth and Electronics
Abraham Steve Colina – Bass and Vocals
