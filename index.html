WHO ARE WE?

A New Day is a melodic metalcore band from South Texas, drawing influence from the emotionally driven and powerful sounds of early 2000s bands like A Long Winter, Hopesfall, and Broken by Silence. Combining heaviness with melody and electronic textures.

Band Members:
Brenden Riker – Lead Vocalist
David Falcon – Guitarist
Aiden Mendoza – Guitarist
Javier Morales – Drummer
George Gonzales – Synth and Electronics
Abraham Steve Colina – Bass
